Apartment for rental at Miraflores Rancho A

Welcome to my privately-owned 2 bedroom second floor apartment on Miraflores Rancho A at Miraflores, Mijas Costa, Costa del Sol, Spain. There is a spacious lounge with dining area and a fully-fitted open-plan kitchen. The lounge leads onto its own private balcony overlooking the pool and has excellent views of the sea.

Miraflores (www.miraflores.com) is one of the nicest developments along the Costa del Sol coastline situated midway between Fuengirolla and Marbella. The apartments and town houses are set in magnificent gardens with pleasant walkways and plenty of car parking spaces. At the heart of the development is the Club Miraflores with its first-class reasonably priced restaurant and live cabaret entertainment. Miraflores is a gated community with 24 hr security. A short walk within Miraflores will take you to the Playa Miraflores beach and to the Sports Complex for tennis and bowls. Nearby is Miraflores own golf course (www.miraflores-golf.com) where buggy, trolley and club hire is available and Miraflores guests get subsidised rates of play. Miraflores is just 25 minutes from Malaga International Airport. Miraflores Rancho A is the most popular community within Miraflores.
